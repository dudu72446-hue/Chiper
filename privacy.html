Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Chiper ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains our information practices.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you register for Chiper, we collect the following information:

  • Email address - Used for account authentication and password recovery
  • Display name - Your chosen username on the platform
  • Profile emoji/avatar - Your profile picture or emoji representation
  • Bio/status - Optional information about yourself
  • Account creation timestamp - Record of when your account was created

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data - Information about how you interact with Chiper
  • Device information - IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Cookies and similar technologies - For authentication and preferences
  • Messages and communications - Content you send through the platform

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Chiper service
  • Authenticate your account and verify your identity
  • Process payments for Chiper Coins and premium features
  • Send you important notifications and updates
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Firebase servers. We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication protocols

Note: While we strive to protect your data, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data - Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correct your data - Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account - Request permanent deletion of your account and associated data
  • Control your privacy settings - Manage who can see your information

5. Third-Party Services

Chiper uses Firebase, a service provided by Google, to store and process your data. Please review Google's privacy policy for more information about how they handle data.

6. Children's Privacy

Chiper is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
